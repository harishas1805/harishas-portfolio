## üé® Branding & Tone

### Writing Style Guidelines:
1. **Professional but approachable** - You're a student, but competent
2. **Action-oriented** - Use verbs like "Developed," "Built," "Implemented"
3. **Quantify when possible** - "Improved accuracy by 15%", "Processed 100K+ records"
4. **Focus on impact** - What problem did you solve? What was the result?
5. **Be specific** - Don't just say "machine learning," say which algorithms
6. **Show growth** - Highlight learning and progression

## üåü Tips for Standing Out

### 1. Quality Over Quantity
- Better to have 4 excellent projects than 10 mediocre ones
- Focus on projects that solve real problems

### 2. Show Your Process
- Include project documentation in GitHub
- Add screenshots/demos to repositories
- Explain your thought process in descriptions

### 3. Keep It Updated
- Add new projects as you complete them
- Update skills as you learn
- Refresh certifications regularly

### 4. Make It Personal
- Add a genuine photo (optional but recommended)
- Share your learning journey
- Be authentic in your "About" section

### 5. SEO Optimization
- Use relevant keywords naturally
- Create a custom domain (optional)
- Submit to Google Search Console
- Share on social media
